Steps to Make Borscht

  1. Peel and chop beets, carrots, potatoes, onion, and cabbage.
  2. Saut√© onion, carrots, and beets in a pot with a bit of oil.
  3. Stir in tomato paste and cook briefly.
  4. Add broth and bring to a boil.
  5. Add potatoes and cabbage; simmer until all vegetables are tender.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and vinegar or lemon juice.
  7. SServe hot with a dollop of sour cream and fresh dill if desired.
