Kyivski Kotletki

Home Page

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Butter (cold, unsalted)
  • Garlic
  • Fresh parsley or dill
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Oil

Steps to Make Kyivski Kotletki

  1. Mix softened butter with minced garlic and herbs; chill until firm.
  2. Pound chicken breasts thin and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place a piece of chilled herb butter in the center of each cutlet.
  4. Roll and seal the chicken tightly around the butter.
  5. Dredge in flour, dip in beaten egg, and coat in breadcrumbs.
  6. Chill for 15-30 minutes to firm up.
  7. Fry until golden brown and finish in the oven if needed.
