Varenyky

Home Page

Ukrainian dumplings made with soft dough and stuffed with a variety of fillings like mashed potatoes, cheese, or sauerkraut. They’re boiled and often topped with butter, fried onions, or sour cream for a comforting, homestyle meal.

Ingredients

  • All-Purpouse Flour
  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Potatoes (for filling)
  • Onions (for filling or topping)
  • Butter
  • Cheese or sauerkraut

Steps to Make Varenyky

  1. Make dough by mixing flour, eggs, water, and salt; knead and rest.
  2. Prepare filling (e.g., mashed potatoes with sautéed onions or cheese).
  3. Roll out dough and cut into circles.
  4. Place filling in the center, fold over, and pinch edges to seal.
  5. Boil varenyky in salted water until they float.
  6. Serve with melted butter, fried onions, or sour cream.
